Waterfall: advantages and disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
🔹 Waterfall focuses most on a clear, defined set of steps. 🔹 Leaves almost no room for unexpected changes or revisions.
🔹 Makes your team aware of the overall goal from the beginning. 🔹 Focuses very little on the end user or client involved with a project.
🔹 Emphasizes a clean transfer of information at each step. 🔹 Insists that teams wait until step four out of six to test their products.
