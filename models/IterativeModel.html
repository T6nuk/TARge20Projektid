Iterative model

Iterative model: Advantages and disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
🔹 It is easily adaptable to the ever changing needs of the project as well as the client. 🔹 Although cost of change is lesser, but it is not very suitable for changing requirements.
🔹 Testing and debugging during smaller iteration is easy. 🔹 Highly skilled resources are required for skill analysis.
🔹 In iterative model less time is spent on documenting and more time is given for designing. 🔹 Defining increments may require definition of the complete system.
🔹 It is best suited for agile organisations.
