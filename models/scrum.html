Scrum: Advantages and disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
🔹 Scrum ensures effective use of time and money. 🔹 Often leads to scope creep, due to the lack of a definite end-date.
🔹 Scrum, being agile, adopts feedback from customers and stakeholders. 🔹 The framework can be successful only with experienced team members.
🔹The individual effort of each team member is visible during daily scrum meetings. 🔹 Quality is hard to implement until the team goes through an aggressive testing process.
🔹 Developments are coded and tested during the sprint review.
