kanban: Advantages and disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
➕ The model has no separate planning stage, so a new change request can be introduced at any time. ➕ Outdated Kanban board can lead to issues in the development process.
➕ Kanban methodology increases the process flexibility ➕ Sometime Kanban team make the board overcomplicate.
➕ Kanban is easy to understand the methodology ➕ Lack of timing because there is no timeframes are associated with each phase.
